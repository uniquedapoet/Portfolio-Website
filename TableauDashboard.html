About the Project

This Tableau Profit Analysis project was developed to enhance my expertise in data visualization and business intelligence. The project involves a detailed examination of sales data, followed by the creation of interactive dashboards that provide insights into profit margins, sales trends, and product performance.

The dataset, sourced from a fictional retail company, includes various sales attributes such as profit, category, sales, segment, and region. After cleaning and preprocessing the data for accuracy and consistency, I conducted an exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify patterns and correlations.

Features

  • This chart shows the monthly profit breakdown for eah category, allowing users to identify seasonal trends and fluctuations in profit margins. The line chart is color-coded by category, making it easy to compare performance across different product lines.
  • This is a bar chart that shows sales by category. The chart is color-coded by category, making it easy to identify the best and worst-performing categories. The size of the bar indicates the profit margin for each category, allowing users to quickly assess profitability.
